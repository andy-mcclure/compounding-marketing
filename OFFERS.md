# Offer Architecture

---

## The Core Offer: Compounding Marketing System Installation

Design and installation of the In-House Growth Machine inside a client's business.

Built inside the client's accounts, on the client's infrastructure, producing intelligence that belongs to the client permanently. Longview Marketing functions as architect and builder. The client is the owner. When the engagement evolves or ends, the system stays.

**What this is not:** A monthly deliverables package. A campaign management retainer. Access to agency tools and infrastructure.

**What this is:** Permanent capability installed inside the client's business — a system that compounds results whether Longview Marketing is involved or not.

---

## The Marketing Ownership Audit

**The entry point diagnostic.**

Reveals where a business's marketing intelligence currently lives and who controls it. Reviews accounts, platforms, AI tool usage, vendor relationships, data ownership, and content workflows to determine the degree of ownership the business actually has.

**Produces:** A scored assessment identifying gaps between current state and what is possible under a Compounding Marketing model.

**Standalone deliverable** — owned by the client regardless of what happens next.

A roadmap for closing the gaps is a separate paid engagement.

---

## The Winter Readiness Assessment

**Painting contractor entry point.**

Surfaces gaps in the marketing system relative to what it would take to fill a winter calendar with interior painting work — before winter arrives.

**Urgency driver:** Calendar. The window to build the pipeline closes when exterior season ends.

**Diagnosis only.** Action plan is a separate paid engagement.

---

## Engagement Model Principles

- No long-term contracts before value is proven
- No pre-built packages
- Customized engagements built around the client's specific business need
- The agency manages the process — client contributes direction, not coordination
- Client time investment: two to five hours per month once the system is installed
- Operational standard: *How is it going? What's next? Make it so.*

---

## What Andy McClure Is Not Selling

- Monthly deliverables package
- Campaign management retainer
- Reporting service
- Access to agency tools and infrastructure
- Short-term engagement with no continuity plan

---

## The Ownership Commitment

At any point, the client can export everything built about their business — context files, strategic intelligence, frameworks. Portable by design. This isn't a marketing promise. It's a structural commitment baked into how the work is done.
