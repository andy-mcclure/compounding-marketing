## Professional Background

**Engineering foundation.**
Mechanical Engineering degree, Cornell University, 1992. Manufacturing process concentration. Systems thinking origin — the source of the IHGM architecture, process discipline, and insistence on measurable outcomes.

**Early career — operations and management.**
First role: injection molding department manager, three shifts, scheduling equipment and people to meet production deadlines. Rose through shipping, project management, and account management. Managed a tool and die facility of 15 employees through a financial wind-down — asset liquidation, reduction in staff.

Became IT manager during company's first web presence. Managed the first outside marketing vendor relationship he'd ever seen up close.

**Graduate education.**
MBA — University of Rochester, 70% scholarship, top 10 of 250 graduates. Dual concentration in marketing and strategy — discovered organically. The methodology wasn't designed. It was discovered.

**Sun Microsystems.**
Marketing Product Manager, Bay Area, 1999. Front-row seat to how a category-defining technology company approached market development.

**Software company turnaround.**
Director of North American Sales for a $15M software company. Seven years stabilizing a revenue decline, working with teams in England, Germany, France, India, Malaysia, Singapore. The company was acquired in 2009.

**Longview Marketing.**
Founded 2009 — the deliberate choice of fractional over W-2 at the moment of layoff. Originally Sherpa Business Development. Within months: five or six clients contracting as a marketing leader. Fractional CMO before the term existed.

**17 years. 15+ years serving local service businesses. 12+ year client relationship. Seven concurrent clients.**

**The book.** *The In-House Growth Machine: Build Your Own AI-Powered Marketing System and Escape the Agency Trap.* Published 2025.
